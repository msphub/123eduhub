ABOUT US

EDULEARN

EduLearn is an Non Governmental Organisation.The objective of the Eduhub is to help the students who are seeking for projects in the technical field.

This organisation simplifies the process of obtaining a project by directly contacting the experienced professionals in that particular domain.This organisation provides a direction to the student in a very exquisite way by providing the student all the components of the specific project at their doorstep. We have often observed that students mostly face difficulties while obtaining components for their respective projects, in order to curb this issue we will be having a home-delivery system of all the components along with the professional who will accompany the student with the project at the doorstep. Along with that, we will be offering various technical courses to applicants. This organisation believes in curbing the disgraceful issues being faced by the students in day to day life. We have been into the shoes of the students and understand all the issues faced by the students, therefore we will be having a call support, where students can discuss their issues. Our motto is to make Life Simple and Easier.
